Search genes and features

Find annotated genes or features and review their linked G4 and i-motif sites.

Open gene search arrow_forward

What to enter

  • tag Feature ID, gene ID, gene symbol, or locus tag.
  • edit Examples: ATCG0001, gene-ATP6, TP53, or BRCA1.

What you will see

  • table_view Feature rows with gene ID, assembly, sequence record, biotype, and genomic range.
  • hub The number of linked G4 and i-motif sites for each matching feature.

  1. Search a gene or feature

    Search by the gene or feature identifier used in the genome annotation.

  2. Review feature rows

    Use the columns to confirm that the result is the feature you need.

  3. Open the linked record

    Move from a feature result to its genome or gene detail page.
