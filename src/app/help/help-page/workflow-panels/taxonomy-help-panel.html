Browse by taxonomy

Start with a taxonomic group, then find genome assemblies linked to that taxon.

Open taxonomy arrow_forward

What to enter

  • edit Scientific name, common name, or a short taxon keyword.
  • tag NCBI Taxonomy ID, such as 3702 for Arabidopsis thaliana.

What you will see

  • format_list_bulleted Matching taxa with scientific names, ranks, and NCBI Taxon IDs.
  • storage Taxon records with genome assemblies and links to G4/i-motif data.

  1. Search a taxon

    Search by species, genus, family, taxonomic group, or Taxon ID.

  2. Open the taxon record

    Select the best match from the search suggestions.

  3. Move to genomes

    Use a taxon with assemblies as the starting point for genome review.
