What to enter

  • tag Assembly accession, such as GCF_000001735.4.
  • badge Assembly name, such as TAIR10.1 or GRCh38.p14.
  • science Organism name, such as Arabidopsis thaliana or Homo sapiens.

What you will see

  • table_view Recommended assemblies with sequence-record counts and predicted motif counts.
  • open_in_new A link to the genome detail page for the selected assembly.
