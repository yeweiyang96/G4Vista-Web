Analyze G4 and i-motif sites

Review predicted G-quadruplex (G4) and i-motif sites for a selected genome assembly.

Open example genome arrow_forward

What to enter

  • dns Sequence or region, such as a chromosome or scaffold record.
  • filter_alt Motif type, gene relation, G-tetrads, and score range.
  • abc Optional gene or feature context when you need motif sites near annotations.

What you will see

  • donut_large G4 and i-motif counts summarized by genomic context.
  • table_view Motif rows with sequence, start, end, length, score, and sequence text.
  • open_in_browser Range chart and genome browser views for checking genomic positions.

  1. Confirm the assembly

    Check that the organism, accession, release date, and sequence records are correct.

  2. Filter motif sites

    Narrow the table before you inspect individual sites.

  3. Inspect context

    Use visual views when you need to check where motif sites are located.
